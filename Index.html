The Fence Exercise:

  

You have a project to build a new fence. The fence is four sided. Each side is to take one day to build, and $1000 has been budgeted per side. Today is the end of day 3.

 
           
           
         
           
           
           
  • Day 1 is complete and it was spent $1000.
  • Day 2 is complete and it was spent $1000.
  • Day 3 is 50% done and it was spent $600.
  • Day 4 has not yet been started.
  
Input project data:
What is the total project budget?  
Which working day is you project on?
What is the % progress of fence 1? %   Money spent 1
What is the % progress of fence 2? %   Money spent 2
What is the % progress of fence 3? %   Money spent 3
What is the % progress of fence 4? %   Money spent 4

  • Do the calculations to three decimal places on the exercises.
  • Insert your answers on the fields below and then check your answers by clicking the button "Check your answers"
  • If you need to redo the calculations, please refresh (F5) the page.
